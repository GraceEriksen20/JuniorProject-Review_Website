Welcome to Not a Critic

Welcome to our movie and show review website, built by a group of four passionate students at Oregon Tech. Our mission is to provide Oregon Tech students and beyond with a fun, insightful, and engaging platform to explore reviews of the latest movies and TV shows.

Our Team

We are a team of four students who share a love for cinema and storytelling. Our diverse backgrounds and shared commitment to quality help us deliver content that resonates with our audience.

  • Grace: Team Leader, Developer, Movie lover
  • Christopher: Developer, with a shared passion for cinematic enjoyment.
  • Derek: Co-developer, avid bad horror movie enjoyer
  • Andrew: Co-editor and co-coder with values and love for cinema to make this site one for the lovers alike.

Our Vision

Our goal is to create a space where Oregon Tech students and many others can find trustworthy reviews, discover new favorites, and even share their opinions through comments and ratings. We believe in celebrating the art of storytelling through movies and shows while fostering a community of enthusiasts.

Join Us

Whether you're looking for recommendations, want to contribute your own reviews, or just need a place to unwind with great content, we've got you covered. Let's make watching movies and shows an even more enjoyable experience—together.
